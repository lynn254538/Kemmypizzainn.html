About Kemmy Pizza Inn

Kemmy Pizza Inn is your go-to spot for mouthwatering, freshly baked pizzas made with love and passion. We use the finest ingredients, hand-tossed dough, and our signature sauces to bring you unforgettable flavor in every bite.

Founded in 2025, Kemmy Pizza Inn started as a small kitchen dream and has grown into a beloved community favorite. Whether you dine in, take out, or order online, we promise freshness, quality, and a warm smile every time.

Our chefs continuously experiment with new recipes, ensuring that every slice tells a delicious story. 🍕❤
